Sep-TFAnet: Single-Microphone Speaker Separation and Voice Activity Detection in Noisy and Reverberant Environments

audios


Speech separation involves extracting an individual speaker's voice from a multi-speaker audio signal. The increasing complexity of real-world environments, where multiple speakers might converse simultaneously, underscores the importance of effective speech separation techniques. This work presents a single-microphone speaker separation network combined with voice activity detection aiming at noisy and reverberant environments, Separation TF Attention Network with VAD (Sep-TFAnet\textsuperscript{VAD}).
